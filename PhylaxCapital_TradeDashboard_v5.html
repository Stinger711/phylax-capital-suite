Black-76 Intermediates — Step-by-Step (Auditable) ▼ Show
ALSI Spot (S)—Current index
Forward (F)—S × e^((r−q)t)
Strike (K)—S × (1 − OTM%)
TTE (t)—Days ÷ 365
JIBAR (r)—Risk-free proxy
Vol (σ)—SAVI ATM
Div Yield (q)—~3.8% p.a. (priced into F)
d1—(ln(F/K)+(σ²/2)t)/(σ√t)
d2—d1 − σ√t
N(d1)—Cum. normal
N(d2)—Cum. normal
N(−d2)—Used in put price
n(d1)—PDF — used in Greeks
Put Price (pts)—e^(−rt)[KN(−d2)−FN(−d1)]
Put Price (R)—pts × R10 SAFEX multiplier
Delta (Δ)—−e^(−rt)N(−d1)
Theta (annual)—Black-76 theta (two rate terms)
Theta (daily)—Annual ÷ 365
Gamma—n(d1)e^(−rt)/(Fσ√t)
Vega (R/1% vol)—per contract
Remaining Option Budget—Quarterly budget − premiums paid
Contracts (budget)—Budget ÷ put price
Contracts (carry-neutral)—Yield ÷ |theta|/contract
Recommended—Min(budget, carry-neutral)
