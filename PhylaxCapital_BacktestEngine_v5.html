90/10 Capital Guarantee Mechanics
Step 1 — Fixed Income Leg
FI = Capital ÷ (1 + JIBAR)
Invest the present value of capital into a JIBAR money market note. This PV calculation ensures the FI leg matures to exactly 100% of capital at year-end, regardless of ALSI performance.
Step 2 — Option Budget
Budget = Capital − FI
= Capital × JIBAR ÷ (1+JIBAR)
The residual after discounting = the year's JIBAR interest. This is your option sleeve. Spending it costs nothing beyond foregone upside — the FI leg covers it exactly.
Step 3 — Put Overlay
Strategy Return = Put Payoff ÷ Capital
Worst case = 0% (capital floor)
Buy SAFEX ALSI OTM puts with the budget. If ALSI falls through the strike: puts pay large multiples of the budget. If ALSI is flat or up: puts expire worthless, capital returned exactly.
